Title:
Gahu
Creator:
Felix Akusa Adjetey Sowah
Date Created:

Description:
The Teshie-Nungua Cultural Dance Troupe performs the Central Eʋe dance, Gahu. The ensemble is (left to right, back to front): ƞono, shekeshe, Eʋe sogo, Eʋe kidi, Eʋe kagan.
